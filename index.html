5. What Happens Next?

Our process is simple and transparent, designed to get you recognized fast without overwhelming technical hurdles:

  1. Register Your Asset: Complete a quick form to list what you have already built.
  2. Receive Registry ID: Your profile is logged and assigned an official tracking code.
  3. Asset Classification: Our team reviews your entry and classifies its scale.
  4. Development Screening: Your project is checked for advanced performance value.
  5. Opportunity Review: Explore future options to track and grow your returns.
